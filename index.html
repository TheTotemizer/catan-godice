New game

Set up players, pair your GoDice, and play. The app tracks rolls, the robber, turn timers, and player aids — your physical board does the rest.

Heads up: Live dice integration uses Web Bluetooth. It works in Chrome / Edge on Android, Windows, Mac, and Linux. iPad / iPhone Safari is not supported — you can still play with the manual-entry fallback.

Game setup

Who's playing?

3–6 players. Drag to reorder turn order if needed (just rename them in turn order for now).

Variants & house rules

Pair your GoDice

Pair two dice as your production pair (one red, one yellow is traditional). You can also pair extras and use them later. Each die opens its own browser pairing dialog.

Don't have GoDice handy? (manual entry)

Tap the big dice in-game to enter rolls manually. Useful on iPad, or to test the app without your dice nearby.

Assign dice roles

Pick which two dice produce resources. Any extras can sit on the table.

      VP from settlements/cities/longest road/largest army/dev cards. First to 10 wins.

      Players holding more than 7 cards must discard half (round down). Tap each player's card count.
